My village name is Raithal and it is located in Uttarkashi, Uttarakhand. It falls in the taknaur range of Uttarkashi and its tehsil is bhatadi :Bhatwari. Raithal contains approx 350 families and is considered as a big and developing village of the area. The village famous for its ritiual belief and as a spiritual place.
